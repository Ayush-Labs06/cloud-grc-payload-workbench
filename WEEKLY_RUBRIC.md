# Weekly Review Rubric

Use this rubric at the end of every week. Total score: 100.

## Score Breakdown

- Artifact completed: /20
- Manual debugging quality: /20
- Evidence quality: /15
- Conceptual explanation: /15
- Failure handling: /10
- Git discipline: /10
- Carry-over clarity: /10

## Grading Notes

### Artifact Completed

High score means the week produced a concrete artifact that matches the roadmap and can be inspected without verbal explanation.

### Manual Debugging Quality

High score means errors were investigated manually, hypotheses were recorded, and the final fix was understood rather than guessed.

### Evidence Quality

High score means command outputs, logs, reports, screenshots, paths, commits, or pipeline links prove the work happened.

### Conceptual Explanation

High score means you can explain the underlying concepts, tradeoffs, and failure modes in your own words.

### Failure Handling

High score means the artifact fails clearly, exits appropriately, and makes bad states visible.

### Git Discipline

High score means commits are frequent, scoped, readable, and tied to the risk, control, feature, or gap being closed.

### Carry-Over Clarity

High score means unfinished work is named honestly, moved forward deliberately, and not hidden by vague progress notes.
